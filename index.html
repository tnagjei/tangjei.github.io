Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my Pink Princess Philodendron?

It is recommended to water your Pink Princess Philodendron once a week or when the top inch of soil feels dry.

What is the best lighting for a Pink Princess Philodendron?

The Pink Princess Philodendron thrives in bright, indirect light. Avoid direct sunlight, as it can burn the leaves.
